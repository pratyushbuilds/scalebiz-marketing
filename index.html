The Problem

Why More Ad Spend Won't Fix It

Most agencies optimise the ad account. But the leak that's capping your growth usually sits upstream or downstream of it. No budget increase can patch that.

1

Upstream: Your Offer

If the offer doesn't land, better targeting just delivers more people to a "no". That leak lives on your page rather than in your ad account. It's the first place we look.

2

Inside the Ad Account

Sometimes it genuinely is the ads: fatigued creative, broken structure, the wrong optimisation event. We check. We just don't assume the search ends there.

3

Downstream: Your Follow-Up

Leads nobody called. Carts nobody recovered. You already paid for those clicks, and the leak is everything that happens after them.

4

Our Job: The Diagnosis

An agency paid to manage ads sees every problem as an ad problem. We're paid to find where growth actually leaks, wherever it sits, and fix that first.

How We Work

The Method: Diagnose First, Scale Second

The same four steps, whatever your industry. Finding the leak matters more than knowing your niche.

01
Step 1

Audit

Find where growth actually leaks.

We go through the whole funnel (offer, targeting, follow-up, measurement) and pinpoint the constraint that's actually capping growth, not just the one inside the ad account.

02
Step 2

Fix the Base

Repair the leak before scaling traffic.

Whether it's the offer, the landing page, or the follow-up, we fix what's broken first. Scaling a leaky funnel just makes the leak more expensive.

03
Step 3

Build Measurement

Make every rupee traceable.

Clean tracking and honest reporting, so both of us can see exactly what each channel returns. From here on, decisions come from the data.

04
Step 4

Scale

Pour budget into what's proven.

With the base fixed and measurement in place, we scale spend on what's working. And we keep testing, so growth compounds instead of plateauing.
