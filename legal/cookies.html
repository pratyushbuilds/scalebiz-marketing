1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. They help websites function properly, improve user experience, and analyze performance.

2. How We Use Cookies

  • Essential Cookies – Required for site functionality and navigation.
  • Analytics Cookies – Used to measure visitor interactions via GA4 and Meta Pixel.
  • Marketing Cookies – Help us optimize ad performance and track conversions.

3. Managing Cookies

You can choose to accept or reject cookies via your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain site functionalities or personalization.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party analytics or ad tools that operate independently. We recommend reviewing their respective privacy policies for details.
